Park City

If you enjoy ski or snow boarding and shopping centers, you must visit Park City. Not only are there tasty restaurants to stop by, but there is a yearly summer music festival. There are also many hotels and resorts to stay at.

    Why should you visit?
  • The amazing trails
  • Almost 300 inches of snow each year
  • The friendliest staff you'll ever meet
  • The lodges where you can sleep

If you're a hiking kind of person, keep reading in order to find out more! The first popular hiking spot is called Blood Lake Trail which is more for medium advanced hikers. Another trail suggestion is the Wasatch Crest Trail which is best for advanced hikers. Another great hiking spot if the Gambel Oak Loop, which is the simplest of them all.
