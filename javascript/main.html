Baking Cakes

Vanilla Cakes

Vanilla cakes are basic, but they still taste good. It may just be the easiest cake flavor to make.You may think that vanilla cakes just include vanilla. Like any other cake, vanilla cakes include flour, sugar, paking powder/baking soda, butter/oil, eggs, milk, salt, and of course vanilla.

Red Velvet

Red velvet cake is the best cake flavor to buy. It's not basic like vanilla, or bitter like chocolate. There are 2 special ingredients that make red velvet special. Red velvet cakes include flour, sugar, butter, eggs, cocoa powder, red food dye, vanilla, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and white vinegar.
